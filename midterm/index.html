Facts about the shark

  • Sharks have been around for over 400 million years, pre-dating dinosaurs by millions of years.
  • There are over 500 different species of sharks, each with unique adaptations for their environment.
  • Sharks constantly replace their teeth throughout their lives. A single shark can lose up to 30,000 teeth in its lifetime!
  • Sharks have a special sense called electroreception that helps them find prey in murky or dark water.
